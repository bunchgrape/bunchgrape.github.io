Biography

Bangqi Fu is currently a Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong supervised by Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young. Before that, he graduated with B.S.E from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021. His research interests focus on VLSI physical synthesis with GPU acceleration and machine Learning.
