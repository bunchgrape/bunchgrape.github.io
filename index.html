Biography

Bangqi Fu is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. He received his Ph.D. degree at 2025 from the same affiliation under the supervision of Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young. Before that, he graduated with B.S.E from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021. His research interests focus on VLSI logic-physical synthesis with GPU acceleration and machine Learning.
